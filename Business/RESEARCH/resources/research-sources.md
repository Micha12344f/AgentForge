## Source Hierarchy (Mandatory)

When citing statistics or market data, sources must be ranked by credibility. Always prefer the highest-tier source available.

| Tier | Source type | Example | Use |
|------|-----------|---------|-----|
| **1 — Government / Central Bank** | Official statistical publications | BIS, ONS, BLS, Eurostat, World Bank | Market sizing, macro data |
| **2 — Peer-reviewed / Academic** | Journal articles, working papers | SSRN, NBER, Journal of Finance | Theoretical frameworks, verified research |
| **3 — Industry analyst reports** | Established research firms | Gartner, Grand View Research, Statista, IDC, Forrester | Market sizing, growth rates, segmentation |
| **4 — Company disclosures** | Public filings, press releases, website claims | SEC 10-K, Companies House, company blogs | Firm-specific data, user counts, revenue |
| **5 — Reputable financial media** | Editorial content with sourcing standards | Investopedia, Financial Times, Bloomberg, Reuters, The Economist | Context, trends, definitions |
| **6 — Community / Forum** | User-generated discussions | Reddit, Discord, MQL5, Hacker News, Trustpilot | Customer sentiment, competitive perception |
| **7 — Management estimates** | Internal company projections | Business plans, pitch decks | Treat as unverified; always label |

**Rule**: If a claim relies on Tier 5 or below as its only source, flag it as "unverified by institutional data."

## Citation Format — Harvard Style

**Book**:
```
Author, A.B. (Year) Title of book. Edition. Place of publication: Publisher.
```

**Journal article**:
```
Author, A.B. (Year) 'Title of article', Journal Name, Volume(Issue), pp. X–Y.
```

**Website**:
```
Author or Organisation (Year) Title of page. Available at: URL (Accessed: DD Month YYYY).
```

**Company disclosure**:
```
Company Name (Year) Document title [Internal document / Press release / Annual report]. Location: Company Name.
```

**Case law (UK)**:
```
Case Name [Year] Court Reference.
```

**Case law (US)**:
```
Case Name, Volume Reporter Page (Court Year).
```

**Regulatory guidance**:
```
Regulatory Body (Year) Document title. Body Name Handbook/Guidance. Available at: URL (Accessed: DD Month YYYY).
```
