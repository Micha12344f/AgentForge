# Customer Discovery Directive

## Purpose
Run structured customer interviews to validate pain points, understand workflows, and identify the sharpest wedge for AgentForge's first department agent.

## Target Personas

| Persona | Title | Company Size | Why They Matter |
|---------|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| Ops Leader | COO, VP Ops, Head of Operations | 20-300 | Owns cross-department coordination pain |
| Delivery Manager | Head of Delivery, Implementation Lead | 20-200 | Owns onboarding and project handoff pain |
| Finance Lead | Finance Manager, Controller | 20-200 | Owns invoicing, collections, and close pain |
| Founder | CEO, Managing Director | 10-100 | Feels all pains, makes buying decisions |

## Interview Script Template

1. **Context**: What does your company do? How many people? What tools do you use daily?
2. **Pain**: What is the most frustrating repetitive workflow in your operations?
3. **Impact**: What happens when it goes wrong? How often?
4. **Current solution**: How do you handle it today? What tools?
5. **Cost**: How much time/money does this cost per month?
6. **Willingness**: If something could handle 80% of this automatically with human approval gates, would you pay for it? How much?
7. **Trust**: What would you need to see before trusting an AI agent with this workflow?

## Output

After each interview, log:
- Pain points discovered (ranked by severity)
- Tools currently used
- Budget indication
- Trust requirements
- Quotable soundbites

Goal: 5+ interviews completed by Day 12.
