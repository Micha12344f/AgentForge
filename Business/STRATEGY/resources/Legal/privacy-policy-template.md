## 2. What Data We Collect

### 2.1 Data You Provide
- **Account information**: Email address and password when you register
- **Profile information**: Name and phone number (optional)
- **Payment information**: Processed by our payment provider Creem.io — we do not store card details
- **Support requests**: Messages sent via Discord, email, or our chat widget
- **Communications**: Emails you send us and your preferences for receiving marketing

### 2.2 Data Collected Automatically
- **Usage data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent on the app
- **Device data**: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
- **Network data**: IP address (anonymised for analytics), approximate location (country level)
- **Cookies**: See Section 8 below

### 2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect
- Trading account credentials (MT5 login passwords)
- Trading positions or financial data from your broker accounts
- Bank account or credit card numbers (handled by Creem.io)

## 7. Your Rights

Under the UK GDPR, you have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data (submit a Data Subject Access Request)
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict** processing of your data
- **Port** your data to another service (in machine-readable format)
- **Object** to processing (including direct marketing)
- **Withdraw consent** at any time (without affecting prior processing)

To exercise any right, email: hedgeedgebusiness@gmail.com

We will respond within **30 days**. If your request is complex, we may extend by up to 2 months (with notification).
