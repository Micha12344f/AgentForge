# FCA Financial Promotions Rules — Hedge Edge Compliance Guide
> Applicable rules for marketing a trade management tool in the UK
> Based on: Financial Services and Markets Act 2000 (FSMA), FCA COBS 4, CAP Code

---

## 1. Does Hedge Edge Need FCA Authorisation?

**Current assessment: NO** — but with caveats.

Hedge Edge is a **trade management tool** (software) that copies/reverses trades. It does NOT:
- Hold client money
- Execute trades on behalf of clients (users run their own MT5 terminals)
- Provide personalised investment advice
- Manage investments

**However**, marketing must be carefully worded to avoid crossing into regulated activities:

| Activity | Regulated? | Hedge Edge Status |
|---|---|---|
| Providing software for trade management | No | This is what we do |
| Advising users on which trades to make | Yes (investment advice) | We do NOT do this |
| Recommending specific brokers for profit | Possibly (if it looks like advice) | Disclose IB relationship |
| Claiming guaranteed returns | Breach of promotions rules | PROHIBITED |
| Operating as a signal service | Yes (if personal recommendations) | We do NOT do this |

## 2. Financial Promotions Restriction (s.21 FSMA)

Section 21 FSMA prohibits communicating a **financial promotion** unless:
1. The person is FCA-authorised, or
2. The promotion is approved by an FCA-authorised person, or
3. An exemption applies

### Does Hedge Edge marketing constitute a "financial promotion"?

A financial promotion is an invitation or inducement to engage in investment activity. Key test:

- **If we say**: "Use Hedge Edge to manage your trades" → **Likely NOT a financial promotion** (software tool)
- **If we say**: "Use Hedge Edge to make guaranteed profits from hedging" → **IS a financial promotion** (inducement to trade)
- **If we say**: "Open an account with our partner broker" → **Borderline** (inducement to use a regulated service)

**Safe approach**: Always describe Hedge Edge as a **trade management and risk reduction tool**, not as a way to make money.

## 3. Prohibited Language

These phrases/claims MUST NOT appear in any Hedge Edge marketing material:

| Prohibited | Why | Safe Alternative |
|---|---|---|
| "Guaranteed profits/returns" | Misleading; no guarantees exist | "Designed to help protect your capital" |
| "Risk-free hedging" | Nothing is risk-free | "Risk-managed hedging strategy" |
| "No-loss trading" | Implies zero downside | "Capital preservation through hedging" |
| "Make money while you sleep" | Get-rich-quick implication | "Automated trade management" |
| "100% safe/secure" | Absolute claims are misleading | "Designed to reduce your exposure" |
| "Financial advice" | We're not authorised | "Trade management tool" |
| "Investment recommendation" | We're not authorised | "Software for hedge management" |
| "Beat the market" | Performance promise | "Manage your challenges more effectively" |

## 4. Required Disclaimers

Every piece of marketing MUST include appropriate disclaimers:

### A. Capital Risk Disclaimer (MANDATORY on all materials)
> "Trading involves significant risk. You may lose some or all of your invested capital. Only trade with money you can afford to lose. Hedge Edge is a trade management tool and does not guarantee any trading outcomes."

### B. Not Financial Advice Disclaimer (MANDATORY)
> "Hedge Edge does not provide financial, investment, or trading advice. The software is a trade management tool. Please consult a qualified financial adviser before making trading decisions."

### C. Past Performance Disclaimer (when showing any performance data)
> "Past performance is not indicative of future results. Any examples shown are hypothetical and for illustration purposes only."

### D. IB Disclosure (when linking to partner brokers)
> "Hedge Edge Ltd receives commission from partner brokers when you open an account through our referral links. This does not affect the cost of services to you."

### E. Prop Firm Disclaimer
> "Hedge Edge is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any proprietary trading firm. Users are responsible for ensuring hedging complies with their prop firm's terms of service."

## 5. Social Media Specific Rules

The **ASA CAP Code** and **FCA social media guidance** apply to all platforms:

### Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn:
- Character limits are NOT an excuse to omit disclaimers
- Use "#ad" or "#capital-at-risk" hashtags where appropriate
- Link to full disclaimer page if space is limited
- Claims must be substantiable

### YouTube:
- Verbal disclaimers required in video content
- Written disclaimers in video description
- "Not financial advice" statement near the start

### Discord:
- Bot responses mentioning trading should include risk disclaimer
- Pin disclaimer in relevant channels

## 6. The "28x ROI" Claim

Hedge Edge marketing uses a "28x ROI" calculation. This requires:

1. **Clear basis**: Explain the scenario (e.g., "$29/mo subscription protecting $10K challenges")
2. **Assumptions stated**: How many challenges, what pass rate, what lot sizes
3. **Past performance disclaimer**: "This is a hypothetical illustration only"
4. **No guarantee language**: "Individual results will vary. This is not a guarantee of returns."

**Recommended format**:
> "Example: A trader running three $10K challenges at $29/month. If hedging saves even one challenge, the potential return significantly exceeds the subscription cost. *Individual results will vary. This is a hypothetical example only. Past performance is not indicative of future results.*"

## 7. Email Marketing Compliance

Beyond GDPR consent requirements:

- Every marketing email must include a working unsubscribe link
- Must include Hedge Edge Ltd company details and registered address
- Must not use misleading subject lines
- Must include relevant risk disclaimers
- "Soft opt-in" applies ONLY to existing customers for similar products

---

## 8. Comparative Advertising

When comparing Hedge Edge to competitors:
- Comparisons must be factual and verifiable
- Must not denigrate competitors
- Must compare like-for-like features
- Source any performance or pricing claims
- Comply with the Business Protection from Misleading Marketing Regulations 2008
