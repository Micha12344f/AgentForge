# Approved Risk Disclaimers — Hedge Edge Ltd
> Pre-approved disclaimer language for all marketing materials
> Review annually or when regulations change

---

## 1. Standard Capital Risk Disclaimer
**Use on**: Landing page footer, pricing page, all marketing materials, email signatures

> Trading involves significant risk. You may lose some or all of your invested capital. Only trade with money you can afford to lose. Hedge Edge is a trade management tool and does not guarantee any trading outcomes.

---

## 2. Not Financial Advice Disclaimer
**Use on**: Landing page, Terms of Service, marketing emails, social media bios

> Hedge Edge does not provide financial, investment, or trading advice. Our software is a trade management tool designed to help automate hedge operations. Please consult a qualified financial adviser before making trading decisions.

---

## 3. Past Performance Disclaimer
**Use on**: Any page showing ROI calculations, testimonials, performance data, or case studies

> Past performance is not indicative of future results. Any examples, scenarios, or performance figures shown are hypothetical and for illustration purposes only. Individual results will vary based on market conditions, broker execution, and other factors.

---

## 4. IB Commission Disclosure
**Use on**: Broker recommendation pages, IB/affiliate links, pricing page broker mentions

> Hedge Edge Ltd may receive commission from partner brokers when you open an account through our referral links. This does not affect the cost of services you receive from the broker. You are under no obligation to use our partner brokers.

---

## 5. Prop Firm Independence Disclaimer
**Use on**: Landing page, product descriptions, educational content

> Hedge Edge is an independent software tool. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any proprietary trading firm. It is your responsibility to check that hedging is permitted under your prop firm's rules before using this software.

---

## 6. Software Disclaimer
**Use on**: Download page, product descriptions, Terms of Service

> Hedge Edge is provided "as is". While we strive for reliability, software may experience bugs, connectivity issues, or execution delays. We recommend testing with small positions before scaling your hedge operations. Always monitor your trades.

---

## 7. Hypothetical ROI Disclaimer (for the "28x ROI" claim)
**Use on**: Wherever the 28x ROI figure is referenced

> This ROI figure is a hypothetical illustration based on the following scenario: A trader paying $29/month for Challenge Shield while running hedge operations on a $10,000 prop firm evaluation. The calculation assumes a specific scenario where hedging preserves capital that would otherwise be lost. Actual results will vary significantly. This is not a guarantee of returns. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Always include at least Disclaimer #1** (Capital Risk) on any marketing material
2. **Add Disclaimer #2** (Not Financial Advice) alongside any educational content
3. **Add Disclaimer #3** (Past Performance) whenever showing numbers or examples
4. **Add Disclaimer #4** (IB Disclosure) whenever mentioning partner brokers
5. Disclaimers must be **legible** — not buried in tiny text (ICO/ASA guidance)
6. On social media, link to full disclaimer page if space is limited
7. Update disclaimers when regulations change or new products launch
