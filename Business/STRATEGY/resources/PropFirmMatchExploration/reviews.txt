Additional Information

The Prop Firm Reviews page brings together verified feedback from traders who have real experience with the firms they review. This helps keep reviews more reliable and reduces the noise you often see on open forums.

On this page, you can compare firms by review volume and category ratings such as trading conditions, customer care, user friendliness, and payout process. Use filters to narrow results by review count, funded-only experiences, and paid-out experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
What makes these reviews "verified"?

Reviews come from traders with verified experiences, helping ensure feedback reflects real interactions rather than general opinions.

Can I filter reviews by funded or paid-out traders?

Yes. Use the "Funded Only" and "Paid Out Only" filters to focus on traders who reached those stages.

Do more reviews mean a firm is better?

Not necessarily. Higher review volume can reflect popularity and scale. Use review quality and category breakdowns alongside review count.

What do the category ratings mean?

Category ratings summarize trader feedback across areas like trading conditions, customer care, platform usability, and payout process.

Can I leave a review for a prop firm?

Yes. Use the "Post a Review" button. Reviews are tied to verified experiences to keep feedback credible.

Why might a firm's rating change over time?

As new verified reviews are added, category scores and overall ratings can shift based on recent trader experiences.
