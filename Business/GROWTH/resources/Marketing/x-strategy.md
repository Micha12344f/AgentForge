# X Strategy

> Reference for Growth Marketing. X is the strongest awareness channel and should be treated as the default social surface for market-facing narrative.

## Profile Setup

- Handle: `@hedgeedge` or closest available brand handle
- Bio: `Automated hedging for prop firm traders | Protect your capital whether you pass or fail | Free guide below`
- Pinned post: hero explainer thread or strongest proof-driven launch thread
- Header: product screenshot, chart-facing interface, or brand hero image

## Content Mix

### Threads

- Educational breakdowns of how hedging works for prop traders
- Founder or build-in-public story threads
- Data-backed market or failure-rate threads
- Format: strong hook, 5 to 12 tweets, concrete value, CTA at the end

### Single Tweets

- Hot takes on prop-firm risk and trader behavior
- Quick educational insights
- Product observations from live testing
- Engagement questions that surface trader pain
- Memes and GIF-led TOFU posts

### Replies

- Engage with prop-firm creators, brokers, and trading operators
- Add value in public rather than dropping links immediately
- Use replies to qualify language that can later become posts or threads

## Funnel Mapping

| Stage | Goal | Best formats |
|---|---|---|
| TOFU | Attention and shareability | memes, GIFs, hooks, quick questions |
| MOFU | Education and trust | stat posts, industry takes, uncomfortable truths, short threads |
| BOFU | Conversion and proof | product clips, screenshots, launch threads, direct CTA posts |

## Voice And Tone

- Direct and confident
- Data-backed where possible
- Slightly provocative when it sharpens the point
- Empathetic to trader frustration
- Never condescending, scammy, or overly polished

## Safety Notes

- Dry-run before live posting new thread structures.
- Respect the 25-second minimum delay for threads.
- After a 403, cool down for 20 minutes before retrying.
- Keep content in files, not inline shell commands, to protect dollar signs and Unicode.
- Do not publish blank meme templates from the library.
