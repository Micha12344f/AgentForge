# Short-Form Social Content Directive

## Purpose
Build audience and trust through daily or every-other-day posts showing what was built and learned.

## Platforms

| Platform | Audience | Tone |
|----------|----------|------|
| LinkedIn | Decision-makers, ops leaders, founders | Professional, outcome-focused |
| X (Twitter) | Builders, developers, AI community | Technical, concise, build-in-public |

## Post Types

1. **Build update**: "Today I built X. Here's what happened." + screenshot/diagram
2. **Lesson learned**: "I assumed X. Turns out Y. Here's why that matters."
3. **Architecture insight**: "Here's how our agent handles approval flows" + diagram
4. **Pain point observation**: "I noticed X is still manual for most SMEs. Here's what we're doing about it."
5. **Demo clip**: Short video of an agent workflow running end-to-end

## Cadence

- Minimum: 3 posts per week across platforms
- Target: 1 post per day
- Best time: capture the insight while building, polish and publish in the last 30 min of the day

## Rules

- Every post must reference something real that was built
- No hype, no "AI will change everything" — show don't tell
- Include visuals where possible (screenshots, diagrams, short clips)
